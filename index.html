About Me

Hello! I'm Jonathan Pak, often called JPak by my peers, and I'm a Seattle-based software engineer with nearly a decade of industry experience!

The majority of my professional work has been as a front-end engineer in web with a focus on React, Redux, and JavaScript for over 7 years and a couple of years working full-stack using Node, Express, and MongoDB. I've had experience in both a large company and at start-ups alike and enjoy the different aspects in both environments!

I've been an avid team sports person growing up and love to work collaboratively. I'm a firm believer in "teamwork makes the dream work" and that the whole must be greater than the sum of its parts! One of the benef I particularly enjoy a mentorship culture and helping amplify others.
